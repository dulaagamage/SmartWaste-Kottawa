What is SmartWaste Kottawa ...

SmartWaste Kottawa is a visionary digital platform developed to tackle the urgent issue of improper waste management in the Kottawa area. This community-focused initiative aims to address problems such as illegal dumping, low recycling rates, and lack of awareness about sustainable disposal practices.

At its core, SmartWaste Kottawa uses simple web technologies to offer innovative yet accessible solutions that empower local residents. The platform integrates educational content, community event updates, a smart dump-spot mapping tool, and a dynamic feedback system using Vue.js — all designed to improve transparency, encourage collaboration, and create a cleaner urban environment.

Whether you're a student, resident, local authority, or eco-conscious citizen, this website invites you to engage, learn, and be part of a digital movement that turns Kottawa into a smarter, greener place to live. This project also showcases how technology and local knowledge can work hand in hand to reshape the future of city life — one neighborhood at a time.
